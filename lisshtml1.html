Brown Butter

What is Brown Butter?

Beurre Noisette

Brown butter originated in France as beurre noisette, which can be translated literally to hazelnut butter.

How is Brown Butter Made?

Brown butter is made by melting, then cooking butter over low or medium-low heat.While cooking, the butter will separate, allowing milk solids to collect at the bottom of the pan. The milk solids will brown as the butter continues to cook and it will begin to emit a nutty aroma, thus resembling hazelnuts in color, taste, and smell, hence the French name beurre noisette.

How to Use Brown Butter

Brown butter can be used in many dishes, both savory and sweet. My personal favorite use is in chocolate chip cookies.
